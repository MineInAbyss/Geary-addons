Component for overriding custom mobs sounds.

Parameters

volume

How loud the sound is.

pitch

The frequency of the sounds.

pitchRange

When playing a sound, the pitch will a random number between pitch +/- pitchRange.

ambient

The ambient sound this entity should make at random intervals.

death

The sound that plays on death.

hurt

The sound for when getting hit.

splash

The sound when falling into water.

swim

The sound for swimming in water.

ambientRate

How often on average should the ambient sound play.
